CarChoice: Discover Your Ideal Four-Wheeler

Project Overview

As international students from India living in the USA, we encountered difficulties navigating the vehicle market in a new country. This challenge inspired the creation of our Vehicle Inventory System, a platform that enables users to compare and explore 4-wheeler vehicles from both the USA and India. Designed specifically for individuals unfamiliar with foreign car brands and models, the platform provides an accessible way to compare vehicle options across both markets.

Key Features

Comprehensive Data Tracking: The system manages data for 4-wheeler vehicles produced between 2010 and 2024, focusing on brands from India and the USA.

Side-by-Side Comparisons: Users can compare vehicle models from both countries, exploring specifications and details for each brand and year.
